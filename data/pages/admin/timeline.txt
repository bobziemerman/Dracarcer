====== Timeline ======

=== Pre-history ===
  * Most of the major races are indigenous to specific regions and evolved to suit their climate
    * Northern Aidilon: Dwarves (harsh; mountainous; cold)
    * Western Aidilon: Halflings (gentle rolling hills; some foliage; readily available resources)
    * Eastern Aidilon: Gnomes (dense woods; small hiding spaces; forest magic)
    * Lower Aidilon: Elves (gentle, stable climate; magic-rich)
    * Southern Aidilon Peninsula: Humans (varied terrains; changing climate; isolated inside mountain range)
    * West Ordan: Orcs (dry; scarce resources; strong predators)
    * With the following exceptions:
      * Aasimar/Tieflings: Deity/Human hybrids; born whenever, wherever
      * Dragonborn: Dragon/Human hybrids; born whenever, wherever
  * Massively powerful creatures beyond mortality called the Maha'ai rampage throughout the world

=== 1st Age ===
  * 1: 
    * The ancient races of Aidilon band together and create lures and one-way gates in the south to banish most of the the Maha'ai to other planes
    * Humans abandon their homeland in the south, spread throughout Aidilon as nomads
  * 135: Dwarven tribes band together under the banner of the Istovian Empire
  * 278: Istovia contacted by Hroknar, the first Maha'ai to reach back from the beyond the gates
  * 283: The Church of Hroknar becomes widespread across Istovia

=== 2nd Age ===
  * 1,739: Dwarves discover a rare, magic-resistant element, Valthril, in the mountains of Istovia

=== 3rd Age ===
  * 2,184: Istovia has a Valthril-fueled mechanical renaissance; invades and conquers most of Aidilon
  * 2,213: Istovia invades and conquers most of Aidilon
  * 2,244: Istovian Empire repatriates its citizens in an attempt to erase all non-Istovian culture
  * 2,689: Istovia formally bans all arcane magic
  * 2,706: Uprisings across Aidilon overthrow Istovia and push them back to the north
  * 2,728: The Kingdom of Evedale, the Realm of Hartland, and the Qurean Polity are all formed to defend against future threats; establish peace treaties with each other
  * 2,980: Drow, now a fully distinct sub-race, are forced into Underdark, take the Bloodwell with them
