====== Sage Vault - Directory ======

This repository of information is maintained by the Sage Guild at a secure location, as a safeguard for the preservation of knowledge and culture discovered by/pertaining to the residents of the continent of Aidilon, in the event of another catastrophic annihilation. 

The books and passages contained in this collection are the works deemed pertinent to a survey of the continent, and any opinions expressed therein are the author's own.
